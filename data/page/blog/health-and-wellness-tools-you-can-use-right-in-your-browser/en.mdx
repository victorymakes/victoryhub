## 🧮 BMI Calculator: Know Where You Stand

**What is BMI?**
BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using your height and weight. It estimates if you're underweight, normal, overweight, or obese.

**Why it matters:**
Sometimes we just want a quick gut-check. BMI isn’t perfect — it doesn’t account for muscle mass — but it’s a useful snapshot, especially when tracked over time.

**If you’ve ever wondered:**

- “Am I in a healthy range?”
- “How has my body changed the past year?”
- “Where do I stand today?”

…it’s a click away. No judgment. Just info.

## 🔥 BMR Calculator: Understand Your Body’s Daily Needs

**What is BMR?**
Your Basal Metabolic Rate is how many calories your body burns _doing nothing_ — just keeping you alive.

**Why it matters:**
If you’ve ever:

- tried counting calories
- used a fitness app
- guessed how much to eat based on a YouTube video...

...you’ve probably misunderstood your own metabolism.

Use this to calculate your actual baseline — and plan smarter.
